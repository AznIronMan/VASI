# Branding Standard

VASI is the product name; the expansion is **Verified Authorized Signing
Infrastructure**. The intended public identity is Clark & Burke/CNB, maintained
by Street Kings Productions.

## Required Consistency

- Use `VASI` in product navigation, page titles, email identity, and deployment
  labels after the relevant upstream surface is approved for customization.
- Use approved CNB logos, colors, typography, domains, legal entity names, and
  support contacts from owned source assets or configuration.
- Keep transactional email, browser UI, completed-document appearance, and
  support links consistent.
- Maintain usable contrast, keyboard access, responsive layouts, and readable
  signature/document controls.

## Trust And Attribution

- Branding must never obscure the requested action, signer role, recipient,
  authentication step, consent, document state, or completion result.
- Do not alter audit facts, certificate identity, validation output, or legal
  notices to make them appear more favorable.
- Preserve all license-required upstream attribution and notices.
- Do not use Documenso trademarks in a way that implies endorsement.

## Asset Handling

Keep approved public brand assets in a documented tracked asset path after the
upstream layout is selected. Source design files containing unreleased or
private material belong in an approved private asset store, not `.private/`
copies that become an undocumented source of truth.
